Controls

  1. Press W for wall
  2. Press R for roof
  3. Press T for trunk
  4. Press Y for Yellow Wall
  5. Press U for a unique block
  6. Press D for dark green leaves
  7. Press G for ground
  8. Press L for light green leaves
  9. Press C for Clouds
  10. For increasing the block size, press Shift + P
  11. For decreasing the block size, press Shift + M
